Cards

Cards provide a flexible and extensible content container with multiple variants and options.

Basic Card Example

Use style = "width: #px" or style = "max-width: #px" after the card class to set the cards' width.

Title

Lorem ipsum dolor, sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Nulla quod perferendis voluptate?

                 
<div class="card" style="max-width:340px">   
    <div class="card-body bg-white">
        <div class="card-title"><h4>Title</h4></div>
        <div class="card-content">
            <p>.....</p>
            <button class="mt-1 btn-blue text-white">Click Here</button>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
