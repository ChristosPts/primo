Tables

Tables are a design pattern for displaying large amounts of data in rows and columns

Use the table* classes class inside any tag.

Example #1

Use the table or table-bordered class for a basic table.

# Header 1 Header 2 Header 3 Header 4 Header 5
Object 1 Item 1 Item 2 Item 3 Item 4 Item 5
Object 2 Item 6 Item 7 Item 8 Item 9 Item 10
Object 3 Item 11 Item 12 Item 13 Item 14 Item 15 
            
<table class="table">
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>#</th>
            <th>Header 1</th>
            <th>Header 2</th>
            <th>Header 3</th>
            <th>Header 4</th>
            <th>Header 5</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td>Object 1</td>
            <td>Item 1</td>
            <td>Item 2</td>
            <td>Item 3</td>
            <td>Item 4</td>
            <td>Item 5</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>Object 2</td>
            <td>Item 6</td>
            <td>Item 7</td>
            <td>Item 8</td>
            <td>Item 9</td>
            <td>Item 10</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>Object 3</td>
            <td>Item 11</td>
            <td>Item 12</td>
            <td>Item 13</td>
            <td>Item 14</td>
            <td>Item 15</td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
</table>

Example #2

Use any styling classes to customize your table.

# Header 1 Header 2 Header 3 Header 4 Header 5
Object 1 Item 1 Item 2 Item 3 Item 4 Item 5
Object 2 Item 6 Item 7 Item 8 Item 9 Item 10
Object 3 Item 11 Item 12 Item 13 Item 14 Item 15 
                  
    <table class="table-bordered table-collapsible-border">
        <thead>
            <tr>
            <th>#</th>
            <th>Header 1</th>
            <th>Header 2</th>
            <th>Header 3</th>
            <th>Header 4</th>
            <th>Header 5</th>
            </tr>
        </thead>
        <tbody>
            <tr>
            <td data-label="#">Object 1</td>
            <td data-label="Header 1">Item 1</td>
            <td data-label="Header 2">Item 2</td>
            <td data-label="Header 3">Item 3</td>
            <td data-label="Header 4">Item 4</td>
            <td data-label="Header 5">Item 5</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
            <td data-label="#">Object 2</td>
            <td data-label="Header 1">Item 6</td>
            <td data-label="Header 2">Item 7</td>
            <td data-label="Header 3">Item 8</td>
            <td data-label="Header 4">Item 9</td>
            <td data-label="Header 5">Item 10</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
            <td data-label="#">Object 3</td>
            <td data-label="Header 1">Item 11</td>
            <td data-label="Header 2">Item 12</td>
            <td data-label="Header 3">Item 13</td>
            <td data-label="Header 4">Item 14</td>
            <td data-label="Header 5">Item 15</td>
            </tr>
        </tbody>
    </table>
