Z-index

Z-index is a CSS property that defines the order of overlapping HTML elements. Elements with a higher index will be placed on top of elements with a lower index. There are 9 Z-index classes (z-) from n4 to 4, n4 being a z-index of -4, and 4 being a z-index of 4. Rows have a z-index of 0, any negative z-index values won't show on them.

                
<div class="z-4 position-a mb-1"></div>
<div class="z-3 position-a ml-2 mb-1 mt-2"></div>
<div class="z-2 position-a ml-3 mb-1 mt-3"></div>
<div class="z-1 position-a ml-4 mb-1 mt-4"></div>
<div class="z-0 position-a ml-5 mb-1 mt-5"></div>
