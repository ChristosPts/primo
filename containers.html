Containers

Containers are used to contain and center content within them.

Container Options

There are 4 container classes.

  • container which has a max-width of 1200px
  • container-sm which has a max-width of 700px
  • container-lg which has a max-width of 1600px
  • container-full which has a max-width of 100%

</div>
